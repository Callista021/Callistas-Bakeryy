My Story

Welcome to Callista's Bakery — a dream baked into reality! Founded with love and passion for creating sweet moments, our bakery is more than just a place for bread and cakes. It’s where family recipes meet creativity and every bite tells a story.

As my first business, this bakery is a heartfelt journey — from testing recipes in my home kitchen to serving our wonderful community. Every product is made with fresh ingredients, a pinch of creativity, and a lot of love. We aim to bring joy, warmth, and a little sweetness to your day.

"Made with love, shared with joy."

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To bake happiness into every product, using quality ingredients and time-tested recipes, while providing a welcoming space for our customers.

Our Vision

To be the go-to bakery in Nepal known for delightful flavors, beautiful presentation, and unforgettable customer experiences.
