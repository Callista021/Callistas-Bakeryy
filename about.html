About Callista's Bakery

We began as a small family bakery with a passion for creating fresh, flavorful treats for our community.

Every day, we bake from scratch using carefully sourced local ingredients. From warm banana bread to elegant wedding cakes, we put love into every bite. Our team also offers catering services and specializes in custom cakes tailored to your events.
